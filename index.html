How to use Instonomo:

1. Talk into your devices microphone and the AI will respond.

2. If step 1. isnt working you may need to click the AI ball once to wake it up.

3. Double click the AI ball to bring up a chat box where you can type youre question incase you dont have a microphone or just dont want to talk.

4. To close the AI click Close Instonomo AI
